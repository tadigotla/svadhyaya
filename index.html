Svādhyāya

A Reflection Journal for Honest Self-Study
१ Description
What happened? Record the concrete facts — what you did, who was there, how long it took, what was happening around you.
२ Feelings
What were you thinking and feeling? Name the emotions honestly — they flag where motivation leaks out.
३ Evaluation
What worked and what didn't? Which techniques produced real understanding rather than the feeling of productivity?
४ Analysis
Why did it go that way? Dig into the root cause — is it a missing foundation, or execution under pressure?
५ Conclusion
What have you learned? Turn the analysis into a crisp takeaway.
६ Action Plan
What will you do next? Commit to something specific and small.

Cloud sync

Sign in with Google to sync your reflections across devices. Reflection text is encrypted in your browser using a passphrase you set on first sign-in — Google sees only encrypted bytes. Lose the passphrase, lose the cloud copy — save it in a password manager. Your local copies are never deleted.
